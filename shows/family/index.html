Family Entertainment in Las Vegas

Las Vegas has a reputation as an adults-only destination, but the reality is more nuanced. Several shows on the Strip are genuinely built for all ages — productions where kids are engaged throughout, parents aren't uncomfortable, and the whole group leaves having actually had a good time together.

Tournament of Kings at Excalibur is the flagship family experience on the Strip. It's the kind of show that works because the core premise — watching knights joust while you eat with your hands and cheer for your kingdom — lands for an eight-year-old and a sixty-year-old equally. The production quality is higher than the medieval-dinner-show concept might suggest, and the included feast makes it exceptional value for groups.

More family-friendly shows are coming to Vegas Sidekick. In the meantime, several shows in other categories are also appropriate for all ages — including Mac King Comedy Magic at Harrah's, Blue Man Group at Luxor, and Awakening at Wynn (ages 5+).
