About BattleBots

BattleBots: Destruct-A-Thon is the only live, ticketed, daily robotic combat show ever created — and it runs every week in Las Vegas. The robots fighting in front of you are the same machines fans recognize from the hit Discovery Channel series: Witch Doctor, Kraken, Mammoth, HyperShock, Whiplash, and others, competing to the death against new challengers built from scratch to destroy them.

This is not a screen. Not a video. Not a reenactment. The crunching impacts, grinding wheels, sparks, and fire are happening ten feet in front of you, at championship engineering levels. When a machine takes a full-speed hit from a spinning weapon, you feel it in your chest. When a robot catches fire or stops moving, the crowd tells you everything — because half the arena just lost.

Pick your robot. Root for your team. Watch the builders on the Robot Driver Stage fight to keep their creation operational under devastating attack. At this level, the skill involved is extraordinary — and watching it live, you understand exactly why this show has built one of the most passionate fanbases in the world.
