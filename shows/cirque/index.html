About Cirque in Las Vegas
Las Vegas has five permanent Cirque du Soleil productions running simultaneously — more than any city on earth. They range from the intimate and classical (Mystère, 1993) to the technically staggering (KÀ, "O") to the straight-up party (Mad Apple). No two shows are the same experience.
