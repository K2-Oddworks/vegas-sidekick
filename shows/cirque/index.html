About Cirque in Las Vegas
Las Vegas has five permanent Cirque du Soleil productions — more than any city on earth. They range from the intimate and classical (Mystère, running since 1993) to the technically staggering ("O" at the Bellagio, KÀ at MGM Grand) to the full-on adult party (Mad Apple). No two shows share the same experience.
