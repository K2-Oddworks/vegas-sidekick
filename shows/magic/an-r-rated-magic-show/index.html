About An R-Rated Magic Show

Looking for magic with a wild side? An R-Rated Magic Show delivers jaw-dropping illusions, hilarious adult comedy, and raunchy audience participation for a night you won't forget.

An R-Rated Magic Show is not your typical Vegas magic act — it's a bold, uncensored, and wildly entertaining ride through jaw-dropping illusions and unapologetically adult humor. Starring the wildly charismatic Grant Freeman, this show combines mind-blowing tricks with stand-up comedy that pulls no punches, serving up a fresh alternative for grown-up audiences seeking something edgier than the norm.

🎩 Bold, Uncensored Illusions
Real sleight of hand and jaw-dropping tricks — not a novelty act. Grant Freeman wraps genuine magic in unapologetically adult humor, giving grown-up audiences an edgier alternative to the family-friendly magic acts up and down the Strip.
