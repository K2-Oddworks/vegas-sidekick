About Allstars of Magic

Allstars of Magic puts multiple world-class magicians on the same stage in a single 60-minute afternoon show at the main V Theater inside Miracle Mile Shops. The format is deliberate — each performer does their specialty, back to back, giving the audience a genuine cross-section of what Las Vegas-caliber magic looks like across different disciplines. Close-up, stage illusion, comedy magic, sleight of hand — what's on the bill rotates, which means the show evolves with its lineup.
