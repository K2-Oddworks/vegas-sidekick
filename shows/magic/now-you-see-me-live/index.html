🪑 Sweet Spot
The front-and-center rows facing the stage head-on — the Sweet Spot for a magic show. Illusions are built to read from a specific angle, and Premium Center puts you exactly where the reveals are designed to land. Close enough to catch the sleight-of-hand details, centered enough to take in the full stage picture. If you're only going to see it once, this is where to sit.
The rows directly behind Premium Center — still straight-on to the stage, just a little further back. General Seating keeps you center and gives you the full width of the production, which for a big-illusion show is a genuinely strong seat at a friendlier price. A smart pick when Premium Center is sold out or you're seating a group.
The angled section off to the side of the house — the budget-friendly option. You'll see the show, but from an angle rather than straight-on, and a handful of illusions play best from center. Fine if value is the priority or you're grabbing last-minute seats; for the full effect of the magic, aim for Premium Center or General first.

Now You See Me LIVE is staged at the MGM Grand Theater inside the MGM Grand Hotel & Casino, 3799 S Las Vegas Blvd, at the south end of the Strip. Enter through the main MGM Grand casino and follow signage to the theater. Guests must be 5 years of age or older to attend, and anyone under 18 must be accompanied by an adult. The show is a family-friendly magic spectacular — illusions, escapes, and audience interaction with no adult content. Kids who love magic, mystery, and big stage spectacle will be captivated. The roughly two-hour runtime is worth keeping in mind for younger children.
The Premium Center section is the Sweet Spot. Magic rewards a clean, straight-on sightline — you want to see the hands, the props, and the reveals without an angle working against you. General Seating behind it still offers a strong full-stage view, and Side View is the budget-friendly angled option.
