About the Show

From the producers behind X Burlesque and X Rocks comes X Country — the newest member of Vegas’s boldest adult revue franchise. Set to your favorite country hits, a cast of stunningly talented and energetic performers brings the barn dance to the Harrah’s Cabaret stage with cowboy boots, sultry choreography, and enough attitude to fill a honky-tonk ten times over.

This topless revue for the 18+ crowd mixes high-energy dance routines with sharp stand-up comedy between acts, keeping the energy high and the laughs coming all night long. Whether you’re a country music die-hard, a fan of spectacular performances, or just looking for the most fun you can have after 10 PM in Vegas — X Country delivers the goods.

💡 Sidekick Note: Harrah’s Cabaret is an intimate venue — every seat has a solid view of the stage. That said, the VIP section gives you the best center positioning with more elbow room. If VIP is available, it’s the move.
