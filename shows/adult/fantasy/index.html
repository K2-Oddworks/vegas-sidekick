About the Show

Fantasy has ruled the Atrium Theater at Luxor for over 25 years — and it’s earned every one of them. Named “Best Adult Revue of All Time,” this topless revue brings 15 electrifying acts to the stage set to today’s hottest music, featuring a cast of stunningly talented dancers and a powerhouse live vocalist who ties it all together.

This isn’t just an adult show — it’s a full production. Sultry choreography, spectacular costumes, and seamless comedy keep the energy high between acts. Whether you’re on a couples’ date night, celebrating with a group, or flying solo looking for a great Vegas night out, Fantasy consistently delivers from the first act to the last bow.

💡 Sweet Spot: Reserve Section 2 — it’s the sweet spot in this intimate theater. Close enough to feel every moment, with perfect center sightlines across the full stage. All seats are good in the Atrium Theater, but Section 2 puts you exactly where the magic happens.
