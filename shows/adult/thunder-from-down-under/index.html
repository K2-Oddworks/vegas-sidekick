About Thunder From Down Under

Thunder From Down Under is the hottest male revue show in Las Vegas — bringing sexy men straight from Australia to the Thunderland Theater inside Excalibur Hotel. These Aussie imports have built a legendary reputation for delivering one of the most entertaining and interactive nights out on the Strip.

Whether it's a bachelorette party, a divorce celebration, a birthday, or just a girls night out, Thunder From Down Under delivers. Expect 70 minutes of dancing, strip tease, elaborate costumes — firemen, military outfits, cowboys, and more — plus a cast of chiseled performers who will find their way into the crowd and onto the showroom tables.

The show is highly interactive. Be ready for six packs for days, iconic Australian accents, and the very real possibility of being brought on stage. Participation is always optional, but the energy in the room makes it hard to say no.

Frequently Asked Questions

Thunder From Down Under performs in the Thunderland Theater inside Excalibur Hotel & Casino at 3850 S Las Vegas Blvd — midway down the Strip between the MGM Grand and Luxor. The theater has its own dedicated entrance. Plan to arrive 20–30 minutes early to clear entry, get to your seats, and settle in before the 8 PM showtime.
All guests must be at least 18 years old to attend. A valid government-issued photo ID is required at entry. No exceptions — children and minors are not admitted under any circumstances. Make sure every member of your group has their ID ready before arrival.
No flash photography and no video recording are permitted during the show. Leave the camera mode off and be in the moment — this is a show that deserves your full attention. Phone use for social media between segments is generally fine, but respect the theater rules during performances.
Thunder From Down Under is highly interactive — performers move through the crowd and onto the showroom tables, and some guests may be invited to participate on stage. All participation is completely optional. By entering the theater, you acknowledge you may come in contact with a performer while seated. If you prefer no interaction, simply decline when approached and enjoy as a spectator.
Category A is the sweet spot — close to the stage for full energy and performer interaction, with better sight lines than Premium for taking in the complete choreography. Category B is excellent value, putting you in the heart of the theater with a great elevated perspective and still well within the interactive zone. Both categories deliver the real Thunder experience. For most groups, especially bachelorette parties, Category A or B is where the night happens.
Tickets are generally non-refundable once purchased, which is standard for Las Vegas live entertainment. Thunder From Down Under runs nightly with a consistent schedule — outright cancellations are rare. In the event of a production cancellation, refund options will be provided.
