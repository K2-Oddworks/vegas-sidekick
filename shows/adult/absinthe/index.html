About Absinthe

Absinthe is a Spiegelworld production that has been running at the Spiegeltent on Caesars Palace's Roman Plaza since 2011 — making it one of the longest-running live shows in Las Vegas history. In that time it has built a reputation as the most acclaimed live entertainment experience on the Strip: TripAdvisor's top-rated Las Vegas show for over a decade, the show HuffPost famously declared "If you only see one show, make it Absinthe."

The format is deliberate and intimate. The Spiegeltent is a custom circular tent holding approximately 571 people, with a center stage and seating all the way around it. No seat is more than 12 rows from the action. World-class circus performers — aerialists, acrobats, strength artists, roller skate acts, contortionists — perform genuine feats of athleticism at distances that would not be possible in a large theater. The Gazillionaire, the show's host and ringleader, connects every act with sharp adult humor that earns the show's 18+ classification. This is not a circus for families.
