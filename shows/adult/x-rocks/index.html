About X Rocks

Turn the classic Vegas topless revue up to eleven, plug it into a wall of amps, and you get X Rocks.

X Rocks comes from Matt and Angela Stabile, the husband-and-wife producing team behind X Burlesque — but it's a different show entirely, not a spin-off. Where X Burlesque plays as a variety-style topless revue, X Rocks is built end to end around one idea: iconic rock and heavy metal songs, reimagined through sultry, high-energy choreography, elaborate sets, and topless dancers who treat the material like a genuine rock show rather than a backdrop for it.

Producer Angela Stabile has described the show as pairing powerhouse rock songs with erotic, high-energy choreography and eye-catching sets — with surprises built into nearly every number. Take that as the mission statement: X Rocks isn't the same revue with a new soundtrack bolted on, it's a full remaster.

A short comedic routine breaks up the run of numbers partway through — a deliberate breather that keeps the 70 minutes from becoming one long wall of sound, and gives the room a laugh before the energy climbs back for the back half.
