About the Show

Chippendales is the original Las Vegas male revue — and after decades on the Strip, it still owns the room. At the Chippendales Theatre inside Planet Hollywood Resort, a cast of impossibly fit performers combines high-energy choreography, world-class lighting and production, and just the right amount of cheeky humor to create a night that stays with you.

This isn’t just a show — it’s a party. The men don’t stay on stage. They work the aisles, they interact with the crowd, and lucky guests get pulled onstage for moments they’ll be talking about for years. Whether you’re celebrating a bachelorette, a birthday, a divorce, or just a wild night out in Vegas, Chippendales delivers every time.

💡 Sidekick Note: This is the #1 show for bachelorette parties in Vegas — and for good reason. The cast knows how to spotlight the guest of honor. Let the box office know in advance if you’re celebrating something special. The performers will handle the rest.
