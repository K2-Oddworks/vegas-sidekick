About the Show

The Wildest Night Out in Vegas

Welcome to the rowdiest riot of drop-dead sexy circus, variety and comedy mayhem this side of the mighty Mississippi. Set inside the real-life Atomic Saloon and starring a rogue's gallery of the city's sultriest, most sensationally talented cowboys, showgirls, lawmen, nuns and ne'er-do-wells, Atomic Saloon Show is a racy, ridiculous romp through the Wild West as it never was.

Uproarious comedy, dancing, circus and acrobatics collide in a fast-paced Vegas vaudeville romp — and every performance is different. The London Times called it "ridiculously enjoyable" and Vegasnews.com raves that the show's "drop dead sexy acrobats and ridiculous comedy mayhem" make it quite possibly the wildest night out in Vegas.

Photography is generally welcomed — just check with your usher when you arrive.

Seating Chart

Know Before You Go

Booths are curved banquette seats wrapping above and below the floor — great sightlines and a cozy social feel. Tables are individual seats to the right of the floor with an elevated side view. Both are Sweet Spot picks.
The Floor circles the center stage with a mix of standing room and bar-stool style seating. It puts you closest to the action but may involve standing for much of the show.
Boxes 50–53 (upper) and 60–63 (lower) are private or semi-private areas at the venue sides — great for groups, though some angles may have partial stage obstruction.
