About the Show

The Greatest Burlesque Show on Earth

Provocative, daring and exciting, X Burlesque delivers a steamy, topless X-perience inside the intimate Bugsy's Cabaret at Flamingo Las Vegas. A gorgeous troupe of dancers performs to a selection of hot contemporary music in lingerie inspired by classic Vegas burlesque — with audience interaction woven into every act.

Known as Las Vegas's hottest topless revue for more than a decade, the award-winning 75-minute female revue ranges from playfully flirty to flat-out X-rated. Highlights include the signature "Champagne Wishes" segment — a classic striptease to Michael Bublé's "Fever" performed in a traditional burlesque champagne glass — plus pole acrobatics, feather boas, bathtubs, cowgirls, 70s mod and much more.

No photos or videos are permitted during the performance.

Know Before You Go

VIP sections (1–5) are the closest seats to the stage and pole — prime position for the full show. Pink sections (2–4) sit just behind VIP and still offer excellent sightlines at a slightly more affordable price point. Both are far better than General Admission.
Bugsy's Cabaret is a small, intimate showroom — even General Admission seats are reasonably close to the stage. That said, VIP and Pink sections put you right in the action. If this is a special night, upgrade to VIP.
Yes — Bugsy's Cabaret is a cabaret-style venue with table seating and drinks service available during the show. VIP sections typically have the best table arrangements and service.
