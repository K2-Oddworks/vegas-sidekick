About WOW

WOW – The Vegas Spectacular plunges you into a world where water, wonder, and world-class acrobatics collide in a 90-minute rush of pure adrenaline and fantasy. Winner of Las Vegas’ Best Acrobatic Show gold medal in both 2024 and 2025, this award-winning production has already amazed more than 2.5 million guests worldwide — making it one of the most celebrated Las Vegas shows running today.

Thirty elite performers — including Olympic-caliber acrobats, aerial artists, and specialty acts — soar, dive, and flip across a custom-built water stage, towering water walls, and wraparound sound that puts you inside the action. In WOW’s intimate 180-degree theater at the Rio, every seat is just feet from the splash and spectacle.

From gravity-defying stunts and high-energy comedy to dreamlike aquatic choreography, every scene is designed to make you gasp, laugh, and leave breathless. Since 2017, WOW has performed over 3,000 shows and earned multiple Best of Las Vegas honors, becoming one of the city’s most beloved entertainment experiences.

What to Expect

💧 Water Stage Spectacle
A custom-built water stage with towering water walls forms the beating heart of WOW. Performers dive, choreograph, and synchronize with water in ways you won’t see anywhere else in Las Vegas — it’s not a backdrop, it’s the star.
🪂 Aerial Artistry
Olympic-caliber aerial artists soar above the stage in jaw-dropping sequences. The intimate 180-degree theater means you’re watching from just feet away — close enough to see the concentration on every performer’s face.
🛼 High-Speed Roller Acts
Roller skaters defy physics in high-energy acts that build speed and tension across the stage. Expect lifts, spins, and stunts that push the limits of what’s physically possible on wheels.
😂 Comedy & Connection
WOW weaves light-hearted comedic moments between the death-defying acts, giving the audience time to breathe and laugh before the next gasp. A perfect pacing that keeps the show accessible for all ages.
🏆 Award-Winning Production
Back-to-back gold medals for Best Acrobatic Show in Las Vegas (2024 & 2025) aren’t given lightly. WOW delivers Cirque-level production values — professional lighting, sound design, and choreography — at a fraction of the price.
🌊 180° Immersive Theater
The Rio Showroom wraps around the stage in a horseshoe arc. Every seat gets a close, intimate view of the action. There are no bad seats — just different distances from the splash zone.
