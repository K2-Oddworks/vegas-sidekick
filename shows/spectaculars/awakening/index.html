About Awakening

Awakening is one of the most ambitious original productions Las Vegas has seen in years — a full-scale fantasy spectacular built exclusively for Wynn Las Vegas. The show runs in the custom-designed Awakening Theater, a purpose-built venue engineered specifically around the production's demands for aerial flight paths, pyrotechnics, and immersive staging.

The story follows a battle between ancient forces of nature. Over 80 minutes, the cast of world-class acrobats, aerialists, and dancers brings that conflict to life through aerial sequences, fire effects, digital projection, and choreography designed at a scale that puts most Las Vegas productions to shame. This is not a revue or a compilation show — it's a fully realized original work with a beginning, middle, and end.

Non-flash photography is permitted during the show. Video recording is not. The production includes pyrotechnics, smoke, elevated sound, and dramatic lighting — guests with photosensitivity, epilepsy, or sensitivity to light and sound should take note before attending.

Frequently Asked Questions

Awakening runs approximately 80 minutes with no intermission. Plan to arrive 15–20 minutes early to find your seats and settle in before the show begins.
Everyone in attendance must be at least 5 years of age. All guests, including children, must have a valid ticket to enter. The show's pyrotechnics and elevated sound may be too intense for very young children — use your best judgment.
Non-flash photography is permitted during the show, provided it's not disruptive to other guests. Video recording of any kind is not allowed inside the Awakening Theater. Leave the selfie stick at the hotel.
Awakening features pyrotechnics, smoke effects, elevated sound design, and dramatic lighting changes throughout the show. Guests with photosensitivity, epilepsy, or other conditions with sensitivity to light and sound should be aware before attending.
Spike's Pick is Sapphire or Ruby — the sweet spot for price and sightlines. Both zones give you a full view of the aerial sequences and stage effects without paying Diamond or Emerald prices. Diamond is the most immersive if budget isn't a concern.
Awakening performs Monday, Tuesday, Friday, and Saturday at 6:30PM and 9:30PM, and Sunday at 4:00PM and 6:00PM. The show is dark on Wednesday and Thursday.
Awakening performs in the Awakening Theater at Wynn Las Vegas, 3131 Las Vegas Blvd South. Wynn is on the north end of the Strip, between Spring Mountain Road and Sands Avenue. Self-parking and valet are available at the resort.
