About Awakening

Awakening is one of the most ambitious original productions Las Vegas has seen in years — a full-scale fantasy spectacular built exclusively for Wynn Las Vegas. The show runs in the custom-designed Awakening Theater, a purpose-built venue engineered specifically around the production's demands for aerial flight paths, pyrotechnics, and immersive staging.

The story follows a battle between ancient forces of nature. Over 80 minutes, the cast of world-class acrobats, aerialists, and dancers brings that conflict to life through aerial sequences, fire effects, digital projection, and choreography designed at a scale that puts most Las Vegas productions to shame. This is not a revue or a compilation show — it's a fully realized original work with a beginning, middle, and end.

🌵
🌵 Spike's Pick — Vegas Sidekick Recommends Awakening is the kind of show Wynn builds when they're trying to remind the rest of the Strip what a real production looks like. If you want to see something that feels genuinely unlike anything else — not a tribute show, not a touring act — this is it.

Non-flash photography is permitted during the show. Video recording is not. The production includes pyrotechnics, smoke, elevated sound, and dramatic lighting — guests with photosensitivity, epilepsy, or sensitivity to light and sound should take note before attending.

What to Expect

🔥 Pyrotechnics & Visual Effects
Fire, smoke, and dramatic lighting are woven into the performance — not just as atmosphere but as part of the choreography itself. The technical execution is at a level you rarely see in Las Vegas.
🪂 Aerial Flight Sequences
Aerialists travel the full height of the theater. The Awakening Theater was built specifically to accommodate the flight paths this show requires — no standard showroom could stage this.
🎭 An Original Story
Not a tribute act or a variety showcase. Awakening tells a complete narrative — rare for Las Vegas productions. There's a beginning, a middle, and an end, with the acrobatics and effects serving the story.
🏟️ A Theater Built for the Show
The Awakening Theater was engineered around the production's technical requirements. Every seat has a purposeful sightline to the stage — there are no bad seats in this building.
🌊 Immersive Scale
Digital projections, elevated sound design, and coordinated lighting make the entire theater feel like part of the production — not just the stage.
💎 Wynn-Level Production
This is a flagship production at one of Las Vegas's most premium resorts. Costumes, choreography, and technical execution are all at the highest level the Strip offers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Awakening runs approximately 80 minutes with no intermission. Plan to arrive 15–20 minutes early to find your seats and settle in before the show begins.
Everyone in attendance must be at least 5 years of age. All guests, including children, must have a valid ticket to enter. The show's pyrotechnics and elevated sound may be too intense for very young children — use your best judgment.
Non-flash photography is permitted during the show as long as it's not disruptive to other guests. Video recording of any kind is not allowed inside the Awakening Theater.
Awakening features pyrotechnics, smoke effects, elevated sound design, and dramatic lighting changes throughout the show. Guests with photosensitivity, epilepsy, or other conditions with sensitivity to light and sound should be aware before attending.
Spike's Pick is Sapphire or Ruby — the sweet spot for price and sightlines. Both zones give you a full view of the aerial sequences and stage effects. Diamond is the most immersive if budget isn't a concern.
Awakening performs Monday, Tuesday, Friday, and Saturday at 6:30 PM and 9:30 PM, and Sunday at 4:00 PM and 6:00 PM. The show is dark on Wednesday and Thursday.
Awakening performs in the Awakening Theater at Wynn Las Vegas, 3131 Las Vegas Blvd South. Wynn is on the north end of the Strip, between Spring Mountain Road and Sands Avenue. Self-parking and valet are available at the resort.
