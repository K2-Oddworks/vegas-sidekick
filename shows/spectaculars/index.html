What Makes a Las Vegas Spectacular

Not every Vegas show earns the Spectacular label. These are the productions that go beyond a single performer or a standard showroom format — fully realized productions with custom staging, world-class talent, and the kind of craft that takes years to build. Each one is a reason to come to Las Vegas on its own.

Absinthe has been running in the same custom circular tent since 2011 and has been the most critically acclaimed live show in Las Vegas for most of that time. World-class circus acts performed at intimate distances, hosted by the unforgettable Gazillionaire. 18+ only.

Cirque du Soleil runs five separate productions on the Strip simultaneously — each built for its own custom venue. "O" at the Bellagio performs over a 1.5-million-gallon pool. KÀ at MGM Grand uses a massive hydraulic stage that tilts to 90 degrees. Michael Jackson ONE at Mandalay Bay blends MJ's music with Cirque acrobatics across a full theatrical narrative. Mystère at TI is the original Vegas Cirque show, still one of the best values at $55. Mad Apple at New York-New York is the edgiest of the five — comedy, aerial acts, and a downtown NYC energy that plays 18+ crowds perfectly.

VEGAS! The Show is the real classic Las Vegas experience — showgirls, live music, feathered headdresses, and the theatrical spectacle that made this city famous. All ages, six nights a week at Planet Hollywood, and one of the best values on the Strip at $55.

Blue Man Group has been performing its unique blend of percussion, comedy, and visual art since 1991 — one of the most recognized live production brands in the world. The Las Vegas run at the Luxor's Blue Man Theater remains one of the definitive ways to experience the show.
