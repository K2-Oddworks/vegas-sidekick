About Vegas Comedy
Las Vegas has a wider range of comedy than most people expect — from Carrot Top's 25-year prop comedy residency at the Luxor to the NYC-imported Comedy Cellar format at the Rio. Some shows are all-ages and perfect for families (Popovich, Marc Savard, Tape Face). Others are strictly 18+ stand-up. Price and age range vary significantly, so check the details before you book.
