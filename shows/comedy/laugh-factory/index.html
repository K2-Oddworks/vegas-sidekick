About Laugh Factory

The Laugh Factory has been one of the most important comedy clubs in the world since 1979. The original Hollywood location launched the careers of Richard Pryor, Jim Carrey, Jerry Seinfeld, and a roster of names that reads like a history of American stand-up comedy. That reputation doesn't travel on its own — but the Las Vegas location earns it. Located inside Horseshoe Las Vegas right on the Strip, the room runs two shows a night, every night, with rotating headliners that keep the bill fresh.

What to Expect

🎤 The Real Laugh Factory
This isn't a brand license slapped on a hotel ballroom. The Laugh Factory on the Las Vegas Strip runs the same rotating headliner model that made the Hollywood original one of the most important comedy clubs in the world. Same name, same standard, right on Las Vegas Boulevard.
🔄 Rotating Lineup
The bill changes regularly — established headliners and rising talent rotate through constantly. Check who's on stage the night you want to attend and come back when someone specific catches your eye. That's the right way to experience a rotating comedy club, and the Laugh Factory makes it easy.
🕐 Two Shows Every Night
8:30 PM and 10:30 PM, seven nights a week. Both shows run the same 75-minute format with different lineups. The 8:30 PM show fits naturally after a Strip dinner. The 10:30 PM show runs later and tends to pull a crowd that's already warmed up. Pick your timing and check the bill for each.
📵 No Photos or Video
No photos or video during the show. Put the phone away and actually watch the comedy. This is standard practice at serious stand-up rooms and it's enforced here. Sit back, enjoy the set, and be present for it — that's the whole point of being in the room.
