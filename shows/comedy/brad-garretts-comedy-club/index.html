About Brad Garrett's Comedy Club

One of the hottest stand-up rooms on the Las Vegas Strip, Brad Garrett's Comedy Club operates inside The District at MGM Grand — a venue built specifically for stand-up comedy. Not a hotel ballroom with a microphone stand dropped in front of a curtain. An actual club, engineered for the craft: sightlines designed for a single performer, sound tuned for a mic and a voice, and an atmosphere that actually says "comedy club."

🌵

Every night features a full lineup in a room that makes comedians sound the way comedians are supposed to sound.

🔄 Rotating Weekly Lineup
The schedule changes every week. Established headliners and up-and-coming talent share the stage — Brad Garrett himself appears on select nights. The show you see tonight doesn't exist next week. Check the current lineup before you book and come back when someone you want to see is on the bill.
