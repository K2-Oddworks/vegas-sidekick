About Comedy Cellar

Thirty-six years. That's how long the original Comedy Cellar in New York City has been selling out every show. The name carries weight in stand-up comedy — it's not a branding exercise, it's a track record. Now the West Coast's first Comedy Cellar location is running nightly at the Rio All-Suite Hotel & Casino, bringing the same format that built the legend to Las Vegas.

🌵
Spike's Take Two shows every night — 7 PM and 9:30 PM. Five headliners each, 15–20 minutes apiece. That's a full night of stand-up for $41. The Rio is off-Strip, which means the crowd skews local and serious about comedy. That's a good thing.

The format is the reason the Cellar has lasted: five rotating headlining comedians, each doing 15 to 20 minutes of their best material, back to back in an intimate 300-seat room. No opening acts running the clock. No padding. Just five professionals doing what they do at a venue built specifically for this kind of show. The lineup changes constantly — established names and rising talent share the bill — which means the room is always running fresh.

What to Expect

🎤 Five Headliners, Zero Filler
The Comedy Cellar format is five rotating headlining comedians, each doing 15 to 20 minutes of their best material. Back to back. No opening acts running up the clock, no amateur hour, no padding. You get 90 minutes of actual professionals doing their jobs in front of a room built for exactly this.
🔄 Rotating Lineup
The comics change. That's the point. Established headliners and rising talent rotate through constantly — which means the show you see tonight is different from the one running next week. Check the current lineup before you book, and come back when someone you want to see is on the bill.
🕐 Two Shows Nightly
7 PM and 9:30 PM, every night. Both run the same format — five comics, 90 minutes — but with different lineups. If you want a choice of time or want to see who's on the bill for each show before deciding, both slots are available. The 9:30 PM show tends to run with more edge.
🚫 No Cameras or Recording
The use of cameras and audio/video devices are strictly prohibited during the show. Smoking is not permitted in the venue. Keep devices stowed once the show begins. This is enforced.
