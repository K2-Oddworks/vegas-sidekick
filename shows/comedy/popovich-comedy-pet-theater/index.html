About Popovich Comedy Pet Theater

Gregory Popovich is a fifth-generation circus performer from Kyiv who trained with the Moscow Circus and toured with Ringling Brothers and Barnum & Bailey. He has performed in 25 countries and appeared on The Tonight Show, Late Night with David Letterman, The Late Late Show, Animal Planet, and America's Got Talent. His Las Vegas show is now in its 15th season at Planet Hollywood — which by Strip standards is an institution.

🌵
Spike's Take Every single animal in this show was rescued from a shelter. They're not wild animals trained under pressure — they're housecats, dogs, and birds who were given a second life and apparently love performing. That backstory lands differently once you're watching them on stage.

The show pairs Popovich's world-class juggling, gymnastics, and balance acts with more than 30 performing animals — cats, dogs, parrots, geese, white doves, and Diamond the Shetland Pony. The combination is genuinely hard to describe and easy to enjoy. It runs 70 minutes at the V Theater inside Miracle Mile Shops at Planet Hollywood, five days a week at 2:30 PM. All ages, no exceptions needed — this one actually delivers on the family promise.
