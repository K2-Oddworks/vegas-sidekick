About Las Vegas LIVE Comedy Club

Las Vegas LIVE Comedy Club runs nightly at 9 PM in the upstairs showroom of the V Theater inside Miracle Mile Shops at Planet Hollywood — a Strip-center location that makes it an easy add to any night out. The format is a rotating lineup of stand-up headliners, 75 minutes, one show a night. Three seating zones give you options from VIP Center up front through the Center and Side sections, all in a room purpose-built for comedy.

What to Expect

🎤 Rotating Stand-Up Lineup
Las Vegas LIVE runs a rotating roster of stand-up headliners. The lineup changes — check who's on stage the night you're attending before you commit to a ticket. That's standard practice for any rotating comedy club and this one is no different.
🗺️ Find the Upstairs Room
The Las Vegas LIVE showroom is upstairs in the V Theater building inside Miracle Mile Shops. Miracle Mile can be a maze — arriving 15 to 20 minutes early is the right call so you're not rushing to find the room at showtime.
🪑 Three Seating Zones
VIP Center puts you closest to the stage in the premium arc. Center section is the mid-floor sweet spot — Spike's pick. Side sections flank left and right with angled views of the stage. All three zones deliver a legitimate comedy club experience; the difference is proximity and angle, not quality.
🚫 No Cameras or Recording
The use of cameras and audio/video devices are strictly prohibited during the show. Smoking is not permitted in the theater. Keep devices stowed once the show begins. This is enforced.
