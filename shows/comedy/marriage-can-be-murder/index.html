About Marriage Can Be Murder

Marriage Can Be Murder has been running at D Las Vegas on Fremont Street since 1993, making it one of the longest-running shows in Las Vegas history. The format is a full interactive murder mystery dinner show — you eat a 3-course meal while the cast performs an evolving comedy-whodunit around the room. Suspects mingle with guests, clues get planted tableside, and the audience has a genuine role in naming the killer before the reveal.

What sets Marriage Can Be Murder apart from generic dinner theater is the interactive structure. The cast doesn't just perform on a stage while you eat — they're in the room with you. What to Expect

🔍 You're Part of the Show
This is not a show you watch from a safe distance. Suspects will approach your table. You'll be asked for your theory. The cast might implicate your group. Lean into it — the more you engage, the better the experience. People who sit back get a fine show; people who participate get a great one.
🍽️ Dinner Runs With the Show
The 3-course meal is served during the performance, not before or after. Timing is paced with the story — don't expect a full sit-down restaurant experience. The food is solid. It's dinner theater done well, not a catered event tacked onto a performance.
😂 Sharp Comedy, Not Cheesy Theater
Marriage Can Be Murder has been refined over 30+ years. The cast knows exactly how to work a room, handle audience reactions, and pivot when an improv moment doesn't land. The comedy is sharp — marital bickering elevated into murder mystery, delivered with genuine craft.
🏙️ Fremont Street Location
D Las Vegas sits right on Fremont Street. Before or after the show, you have the Fremont Street Experience, dozens of bars, and downtown's best dive bar scene all within walking distance. This is the off-Strip Vegas people who love Vegas actually love. It pairs well with a full evening downtown.
