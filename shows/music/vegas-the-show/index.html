About VEGAS! The Show

VEGAS! The Show is a celebration of what Las Vegas used to be — and still is, in this showroom, six nights a week.

The production traces the arc of Las Vegas entertainment from its earliest showroom days through the Rat Pack era and into the modern showgirl spectacular. Live music, big band choreography, aerial acts, and the kind of feathered headdresses that made Las Vegas famous worldwide. All of it in 75 tight, fast-moving minutes at Saxe Theater inside Miracle Mile Shops.

🌵
Spike's Take $55 for the best traditional Las Vegas showgirl production still running. Regular price is $90 — that's 39% off. The Saxe Theater is the right size: intimate enough that you're not watching through binoculars, large enough that the production fills it properly. Get VIP. The stage is low and wide, and the closer you are the more the costumes and choreography land the way they're supposed to.

The Saxe Theater has assigned seating across three tiers: VIP in the curved main floor, General Reserved in the angled upper-left section, and a small number of Limited View seats at the very front of the stage. The show runs Monday through Saturday at 7 PM — a time slot that anchors a dinner-and-show night perfectly. No cameras or recording devices are permitted once the performance begins.
