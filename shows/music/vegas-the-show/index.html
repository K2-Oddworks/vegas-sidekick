About the Show

VEGAS! The Show — The Original Las Vegas Spectacular

VEGAS! The Show is a love letter to the golden age of Las Vegas entertainment. In 75 fast-moving minutes, the show traces the story of the Strip from its earliest days through the Rat Pack era and into the modern showgirl spectacular — complete with live vocals, big band music, aerial acts, and the kind of feathered costumes that made Las Vegas famous worldwide.

The production takes place at Saxe Theater inside Miracle Mile Shops at Planet Hollywood — a mid-sized venue where there isn't a bad angle in the house. The theater's intimate scale means the performers are close. You're not watching showgirls from 200 feet away through binoculars. You're in it.

This is the show people take their parents to, their out-of-town friends who've never been to Vegas, and the one they come back to years later when they want to feel the magic of what the Strip used to be — and still is, on this stage, six nights a week.

🌵 Spike's seating pick: VIP (Rows B–L)
VIP puts you in the curved main floor where you're close enough to see the performers' expressions. The stage is low and wide, so even rows G or H feel intimate. General Reserved works fine for the budget but the angled sightlines from the upper-left section mean you're viewing the stage from the side rather than center.

Frequently Asked Questions

The Saxe Theater is located inside the Miracle Mile Shops — the indoor mall that runs through Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino. You do NOT enter through the hotel lobby. Enter Planet Hollywood, find the Miracle Mile Shops mall, and follow the signage inside the mall to the theater. Give yourself extra time if it's your first visit — the mall is larger than it looks from the outside.
Yes — there are no age restrictions. The show celebrates the classic Las Vegas showgirl era and is a family-friendly production. Costumes are elaborate and theatrical, not explicit. Children who enjoy live music, dancing, and spectacular stage productions will enjoy it. That said, younger kids may get restless during the 75-minute run.
Cameras and audio/video recording devices are not permitted during the performance. Most venues allow phones for personal photos before the show starts, but once the show is underway you're asked to put phones away. Spike's advice: put the phone down for 75 minutes. The production is designed to be experienced, not documented.
VEGAS! The Show runs 75 minutes with no intermission. The pacing is tight and the energy stays high throughout — this isn't the kind of show where you're checking your watch. Plan to be at your seat for the full 75 minutes once showtime begins.
VIP seating (blue zone) is the main curved floor section, rows A through L, directly facing the stage. It's the best sightline in the house. General Reserved (purple zone) is the angled upper section to the left — you'll see the show at a slight angle rather than straight-on. For a show this theatrical, center sightlines matter. We recommend VIP.
Seating opens 30 minutes before showtime. Plan to be inside Planet Hollywood 45 minutes before the show — finding the Miracle Mile Shops entrance takes a few extra minutes if you haven't been before, and you'll want time to get to your seat without rushing. The show starts at 7:00 PM sharp.
