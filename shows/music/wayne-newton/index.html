About Wayne Newton

No living entertainer embodies the history and spirit of Las Vegas more than Wayne Newton.

Wayne started performing in Las Vegas when he was just 15 years old. More than 50 years later, his star still shines on the Strip — his charm and endearing personality as an entertainer earned him a name that stuck for good: Mr. Las Vegas. "Up Close and Personal" is exactly what it sounds like — the legend, a live band, and a room small enough to feel like he's performing just for you.
