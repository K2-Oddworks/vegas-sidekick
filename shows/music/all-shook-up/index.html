About All Shook Up

Elvis Presley and Las Vegas are inseparable — the city gave The King some of his most legendary performances, and it deserves a tribute done right. All Shook Up delivers exactly that: a full live-band show packed with the classics that made Elvis the most iconic entertainer in history. From the shimmy of Jailhouse Rock to the soaring finale of Can't Help Falling in Love, this is 75 minutes of pure rock 'n' roll nostalgia.

Performed nightly at the intimate Athena Showlounge inside Alexis Park Resort, All Shook Up keeps you close to the action in a setting that feels nothing like a stadium concert. The small-room energy means every note lands harder, every hip shake gets a reaction, and the connection between performer and audience is real. Backed by a live band playing the arrangements as they were meant to sound, this is a proper show — not a karaoke tribute.

🌿 From Spike's Notebook

Most Elvis tributes happen in giant casino showrooms where you feel like you're watching from a parking lot. The Athena Showlounge fixes that. It's compact, the sound is dialed in, and you're genuinely close to the stage. If you want to understand why Elvis owned Las Vegas, this is a good place to start.

Whether you grew up with Elvis or you're introducing the music to a new generation, All Shook Up works for every kind of Vegas visitor. No age restrictions, 7 nights a week, and a price that doesn't require a second mortgage.

Frequently Asked

Your tickets are delivered to your email immediately after purchase. Show your mobile ticket at the Athena Showlounge box office at Alexis Park Resort. No will-call line needed.

Alexis Park Resort is at 375 E Harmon Ave, Las Vegas, NV 89169 — just a few blocks east of the Strip near UNLV. It's about a 5-minute drive or rideshare from MGM Grand or the Cosmopolitan. Free on-site parking is available.

Yes — Alexis Park Resort has free parking on-site. Much more convenient than Strip resort parking with no garage fees.

There are no age restrictions — All Shook Up is suitable for all ages. Children under 2 do not require a ticket but must sit on a parent's lap for the duration of the show.

Yes — photos and video recording are welcome. No flash photography is permitted, and no livestreaming. Ushers may ask you to stop if it becomes disruptive to other guests.

Refunds are available for exceptional circumstances such as the unlikely event that a show cancels. Check the full terms and conditions before purchasing.

There's no dress code — smart casual is common. Some guests go full 50s or 70s era in the spirit of The King, which is always welcome. Either way you'll fit right in.

Aim to arrive 20–30 minutes before showtime to check in, find your seat, and order a drink. GA guests should arrive on the earlier side to choose their preferred spot in the showroom.
