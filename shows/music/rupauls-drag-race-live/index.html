All the Drama. Live on Stage.

RuPaul's Drag Race LIVE! has been packing the Flamingo Showroom since 2020 — and it's not hard to see why. The Emmy Award-winning franchise that defined modern drag culture brings its all-star cast to the Las Vegas Strip five nights a week, delivering 90 minutes of lip syncs, original music, comedy, and dance numbers that have no business being this good.

The production is built around queens who didn't just compete on Drag Race — they won it, defined it, and became household names because of it. Asia O'Hara, Jaida Essence Hall, Derrick Barry, Shannel, Kyle Sonique Love, and Jewels Sparkles lead a rotating cast of world-class performers. The lineup varies by show, which means repeat visits consistently deliver something new.

This is not a tribute act or a TV tie-in cash grab. The Flamingo Showroom has been home to residency legends for decades, and this show earns its place in that history.

The Venue

Flamingo Showroom · Table & Chair Seating

The Flamingo Showroom is one of the original Las Vegas showrooms — a classic table-and-booth layout with the stage at the front. Seating is arranged so that most guests have a clear sightline to the stage from wherever they sit.

Seating assignments are handled at checkout. For the best available seats on your date, book early — closer tables fill first. All seat locations are within the same intimate showroom environment.
