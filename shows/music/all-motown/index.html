What to Expect

🎤 Legendary Motown Voices
Big harmonies and powerhouse leads recreating the Motown songbook — The Supremes, Martha and the Vandellas, Gladys Knight, Mary Wells, The Marvelettes and more. These are songs the whole room already knows, sung by a cast that does them justice.
💃 Sparkle & Costume Changes
Sparkling dresses, sharp choreography, and the full Motown-era glamour. It's a visual show as much as a musical one — the Duchesses look every bit as good as they sound, with costume changes throughout the night.
🕺 Get Up and Dance
This isn't a sit-still-and-watch show. The Duchesses want you on your feet, and the room usually obliges. Come ready to clap, sing along, and dance — it's built to be a party, not a recital.
👑 An All-Female Cast
The Duchesses of Motown are an all-female cast whose Las Vegas residency has made them a local favorite. The whole show is focused on what makes Motown so iconic and downright enjoyable — and they deliver it seven nights a week.

🪑 Sweet Spot
The tables in the center of the showroom — the Sweet Spot. You're close to the stage, you've got room for a drink, and you're perfectly placed to get up and dance, which this show absolutely wants you to do. For a Motown party like this one, a table beats a row of seats every time. Book a table and bring a group.
The two Athena reserved blocks directly in front of the stage — the closest seats to the Duchesses. Great if you want to be right up on the performance and don't mind trading the table for proximity. A solid pick for superfans.
Booths 1 through 4 along the sides of the room — a more private, tucked-in option for a small group that wants its own space. You'll view the stage at a slight angle, but you get a dedicated booth and an easy spot to keep your party together.
General Admission at the back of the room — first-come, first-served seating and the value option. Arrive a little early for a better spot. Perfectly good for a budget-friendly night; if you want to be in the thick of it, upgrade to a table.
