BINI Brings Coachella Breakout to Vegas

Vegas Sidekick April 25, 2026 4 min read
"This means a lot to the girls. It actually means a lot to the entire nation." — Lauren Dyogi, Star Magic

The Philippines' history-making girl group BINI is bringing their "Signals" World Tour to Las Vegas following their seismic Coachella debut — emerging as one of the festival's defining breakout acts and the first Filipino group to ever perform there.

Barely two weeks after sparking over 1 million social mentions and trending #1 worldwide on X, BINI takes the stage at PH Live at Planet Hollywood Resort & Casino on Saturday, August 8, 2026, at 8 p.m.

Who Is BINI?

If you haven't caught them yet, BINI is an eight-member girl group redefining global pop with a sound that's equal parts English, Tagalog, and pure cultural momentum. They're the first Filipino act to sell out the 50,000-seat Philippine Arena. They've racked up 2+ billion streams. Spotify named them a Breakout Artist of 2024. And as of April 2026, they're Coachella royalty.

Their fanbase — called "Blooms" — numbers in the tens of millions across TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, and beyond. In any major city, that's a sold-out room waiting to happen.

The "Signals" Era

The tour launches behind their latest EP, Signals — a sonic evolution that positions BINI at the forefront of global pop's rising Asian voices. The project explores sensory moments: the fleeting seconds before emotions fully land. Lead single "Blush" sets the tone. Polished production, layered vocals, English and Tagalog interplay, and a global-facing confidence that feels earned, not forced.

This isn't their first world tour — BINIverse already crossed over internationally — but it is their first following a moment that fundamentally changed their trajectory.

The Coachella Moment

BINI's Coachella 2026 performance was historic. Not just because they were the first Filipino group on the lineup alongside Sabrina Carpenter, Justin Bieber, and Karol G — but because they broke the festival.

Performance clips topped 10+ million views within days. Instagram ranked their set as the most-viewed Coachella 2026 performance by a female act. Google Trends hit a perfect 100 score. Within hours of taking the stage, BINI had more social mentions than most headliners.
