for KING + COUNTRY is bringing their The Most Beautiful Colours Tour to The Pearl Concert Theater at Palms Casino Resort on Thursday, October 8, 2026. Tickets start at $40 (plus applicable service charges) and go on general sale Friday, May 22 at 10 a.m. PT through AXS.com.

Brothers Joel and Luke Smallbone have spent the better part of two decades building one of the most respected live acts in contemporary music. Four GRAMMY® Awards, 13 No. 1 hits, and more than 5 billion career streams — the numbers are striking, but they don't capture what a for KING + COUNTRY show actually is. These aren't concerts so much as productions: cinematic staging, explosive energy, and a message that resonates with anyone who's ever needed to feel less alone in a room full of strangers.

"We've always believed music has the power to remind people they're not alone. This tour is about bringing light into the middle of whatever people are carrying and celebrating hope, redemption and the beauty that can still be found even in difficult seasons. We truly can't wait to experience these nights together."

— Joel & Luke Smallbone, for KING + COUNTRY

What to Expect

The Most Beautiful Colours Tour promises fan favorites alongside new music, all wrapped in the kind of large-scale production the duo is known for. Their recent album What Are We Waiting For? debuted in the Top 10 on the Billboard 200, and they're heading into this run fresh off completing their debut feature film Unsung Hero (Lionsgate) — which won a GMA Dove Award for Feature Film of the Year — and ahead of their upcoming musical film Drummer Boy.

The Pearl at Palms is a 2,500-capacity room that punches well above its size — great sightlines, excellent sound, and intimate enough that you feel the energy in a way you don't at arena shows. It's a well-matched room for a duo whose live reputation is built on connection as much as spectacle.

"The Pearl is one of the most underrated rooms in Las Vegas — close enough to feel every moment, big enough for a real production. for KING + COUNTRY puts on a show that earns that room. If you know their music, this is a no-brainer. If you don't, October 8 is a solid introduction."

— Kris Kidd, Vegas Sidekick
