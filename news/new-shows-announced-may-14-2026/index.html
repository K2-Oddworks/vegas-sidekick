Eagles at the Sphere

The Eagles are coming to the Sphere for six dates across September and November — September 18, 19 and November 13, 14, 27, and 28. It's a Sphere residency, which means the experience will go well beyond the music. Tickets are available at eagles.com.

Matt Rife's Stay Golden World Tour lands at Dolby Live at Park MGM on December 4. At 5,200 seats, it's a significant room — and given his track record selling out Madison Square Garden twice, it won't stay available long. On sale Friday, May 15 at 10 a.m. PT. We covered the full announcement here.

"Eagles at the Sphere is the headline, and it deserves to be — that's a residency that changes the fall calendar for this entire city. But the thing worth noticing is what's happening around it. Brooklyn Bowl, House of Blues, Pearl, Palazzo — all picking up simultaneously. That kind of depth across multiple rooms at once is what a healthy Vegas market looks like. If you've been waiting to book a fall trip, this week gave you a lot of reasons to stop waiting."
Kris Kidd — Founder, Vegas Sidekick. Entertainment industry veteran.
