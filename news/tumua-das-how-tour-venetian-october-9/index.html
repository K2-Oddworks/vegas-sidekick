Comedian Tumua is heading back to Las Vegas — and this time he's bringing the Das' How Tour to The Palazzo Theatre inside The Venetian Resort on Friday, October 9, 2026 at 9:30 p.m. Tickets start at $45.99 plus applicable fees and go on general sale Friday, May 22 at 10 a.m. PT.

Tumua has built one of the more remarkable grassroots followings in stand-up comedy — 800,000-plus combined followers across Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and YouTube, a fanbase that stretches well beyond his home state of Hawaii into Polynesian communities around the world. In 2025 alone he performed in 23 cities across 14 states, 5 islands, and 2 countries. The Palazzo Theatre is the right scale for what he does: intimate enough to feel the crowd, big enough to fit the energy.

You might also recognize him from the big screen: Tumua appeared in Disney's live action Lilo & Stitch, which only adds to what's already a multi-platform career spanning stand-up, podcasting, acting, and social media. His two YouTube specials — Strictly Aloha and Talk Story: The Crowd Work Special — give you a good read on what to expect live: loose, high-energy, deeply rooted in his Hawaiian upbringing, and very good at working a room.

What to Expect

The Das' How Tour is a continuation of what Tumua has been building since he started selling out clubs, theaters, and arenas nationally. His comedy draws heavily from his Oahu upbringing and Polynesian identity — culturally specific in the best way, meaning it hits harder for people who know the references and still lands for everyone else. The crowd work special is worth watching beforehand if you want to know how he handles a live room.

"Tumua is the kind of act that punches well above his mainstream name recognition. If you've seen the Lilo & Stitch cameo or followed him online, you already know — he's genuinely funny. The Palazzo Theatre is a great room for this: late show, Friday night, Vegas crowd. That's the right combination for a comedian who gets better the more a crowd is with him."

— Kris Kidd, Vegas Sidekick
