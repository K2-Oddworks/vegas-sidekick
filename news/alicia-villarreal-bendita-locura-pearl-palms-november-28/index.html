Alicia Villarreal — "La Jefa," "La Güerita Consentida," one of the most beloved voices in regional Mexican music — is bringing her brand-new Bendita Locura Tour to The Pearl Concert Theater at Palms Casino Resort on Saturday, November 28, 2026 at 8 p.m. Tickets start at $39.50 plus applicable fees and go on general sale Thursday, June 18 at 10 a.m. PT at AXS.com. The Citi cardholder presale opens tomorrow, June 16.

The Monterrey-born artist has built one of the most enduring careers in the history of Latin music — spanning mariachi, norteño, banda, and pop, with a catalog that spans decades and a live show that's been known to stop rooms. The Bendita Locura Tour is described as a celebration of her life, her legacy, and the unbreakable bond she shares with the fans who have followed her through every era of her career.

The Bendita Locura Tour

The Bendita Locura Tour is a brand-new production that promises an energetic and deeply emotional experience — part tribute to her legacy, part showcase for her enduring range across mariachi and norteño music. The show is designed to bring the full passion of her catalog to life on an international stage, drawing on hits from across her entire career.

The set will revisit the unforgettable classics that defined a generation as well as the massive solo hits from the era that began in 2000, the period during which she established herself as a solo artist, songwriter, and independent producer. It's a show built for the fans who have been with her from the beginning and for everyone who has discovered her catalog in the years since.
