Backstreet Boys to Headline Official F1® Afterparty at Sphere — November 21

The night the Las Vegas Grand Prix ends, the party moves to Sphere — and AJ, Howie, Nick, Kevin, and Brian are headlining. Here's everything you need to know about the most ambitious post-race show in F1 history.

Kris Kidd • July 17, 2026 • Vegas Sidekick

The Backstreet Boys return to Sphere for the Official F1® Afterparty, November 21, 2026. Photo courtesy of Sphere Entertainment Co.

Las Vegas just raised the stakes on post-race celebrations. The Formula 1 Heineken Las Vegas Grand Prix and Sphere Entertainment Co. have announced a first-of-its-kind partnership: the F1® Afterparty, the Official Post-Race Show of the Las Vegas Grand Prix, headlined by the Backstreet Boys on Saturday, November 21, 2026 at Sphere.

The race weekend runs November 19–21, and when the checkered flag drops, tens of thousands of fans will head straight to Sphere — arguably the most technologically advanced entertainment venue ever built — to catch AJ McLean, Howie Dorough, Nick Carter, Kevin Richardson, and Brian Littrell perform inside the 160,000 square-foot LED exosphere that's already transformed what live music looks like.

Why This Is a Big Deal

The Backstreet Boys aren't strangers to Sphere. Their sold-out residency run at the venue proved that the group's catalog — from I Want It That Way to Everybody — translates into exactly the kind of immersive, visually-spectacular experience that Sphere was built to deliver. Now they're doing it again, on arguably the biggest entertainment night Las Vegas will see all year.

The Las Vegas Grand Prix is a different kind of F1 race. The Strip circuit winds through the heart of the city, with Sphere looming as a backdrop visible from the grandstands. Pairing the race's post-night with a Backstreet Boys show inside that same building isn't just a marketing move — it's the kind of only-in-Vegas collision of worlds that makes the Grand Prix a destination event for people who've never followed F1 in their lives.

"Formula 1 weekend is already the hardest ticket in the city. Closing out race night with the Backstreet Boys inside Sphere turns this into the kind of event people plan their entire year around. If you're on the fence, get off it — packages like this don't last."

— Kris Kidd, Vegas Sidekick Founder & CXO

Sphere towers over the Las Vegas Grand Prix circuit. Photo courtesy of Formula 1 / Las Vegas Grand Prix.

What the Executives Are Saying

"Las Vegas is the only place in the world where Formula 1, the Backstreet Boys and Sphere can come together for one unforgettable night," said Emily Prazer, President and CEO of Las Vegas Grand Prix, Inc. "F1 Afterparty reflects what makes the Las Vegas Grand Prix so distinctive, giving fans the opportunity to see an iconic group like the Backstreet Boys perform at Sphere as part of a race weekend experience you can't find anywhere else."

Sphere's Jen Koester, President & Chief Operating Officer, added: "F1 Afterparty will feature the Backstreet Boys, whose critically acclaimed, sold-out Sphere performances exemplify our commitment to bringing iconic artists together with groundbreaking technology to create experiences only Sphere can deliver."

The Bigger Picture for Las Vegas

The F1® Afterparty concept is a natural evolution of what the Las Vegas Grand Prix has been building toward. The race doesn't just attract motorsport fans — it draws music fans, nightlife crowds, and international visitors who come for the total Las Vegas experience. Anchoring the post-race night at Sphere, with a group as globally recognizable as the Backstreet Boys, extends the weekend's appeal dramatically.

It also continues Sphere's pattern of pairing iconic acts with the venue's immersive technology. If you haven't experienced a show at Sphere yet, this is an extraordinary entry point. The scale of what happens inside that building — 160,000 square feet of programmable LED, the world's highest-resolution LED screen, spatial audio that tracks you to your seat — has to be seen to be believed. The Backstreet Boys' previous Sphere run demonstrated how well their catalog works in that environment. Expect this show to be even bigger.

November 19–21 is going to be one of the most booked weekends in Las Vegas history. If you're planning to be in town for the Grand Prix, adding the F1® Afterparty to your itinerary is a no-brainer. These tickets will move fast.
