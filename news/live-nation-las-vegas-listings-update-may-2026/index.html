Live Nation Las Vegas:
Major New Show Announcements
as of May 7, 2026

by Kris Kidd May 7, 2026 8 min read

Las Vegas know-it-all. Entertainment industry veteran. Founder, Vegas Sidekick.

Metallica is bringing its first-ever residency to Sphere. BTS returns to Allegiant Stadium for four nights. LISA makes Las Vegas history as the first K-pop artist to headline The Colosseum at Caesars Palace. Here's everything newly confirmed.

Live Nation Las Vegas dropped its most comprehensive show update of the year on Thursday, and the list is significant. Among the newly confirmed announcements: Metallica's long-rumored Sphere residency is officially real, BTS is returning to Allegiant Stadium for four nights in May, and LISA is set to become the first K-pop artist in history to headline The Colosseum at Caesars Palace.

The full update spans shows running from this month through December 2026 — and into 2027 — across every major Las Vegas venue. What follows is a rundown of the most significant newly announced additions. Vegas Sidekick does not have tickets to these events. For any of the shows below, check directly with the official venue box office before purchasing anywhere.

The Headline Announcements

● Sphere
Metallica: Life Burns Faster
Sphere · Las Vegas
Oct 1 – Nov 7, 2026 · Jan 28 – Mar 13, 2027
The most anticipated residency announcement of the year. Metallica debuts their Life Burns Faster residency at Sphere — their first extended Las Vegas run — with No Repeat Weekends, meaning the band plays a completely different setlist Thursday and Saturday throughout the run. 24 shows spanning October 2026 through March 2027. Sphere's immersive technologies will deliver what the band calls an entirely new Metallica experience.
● Allegiant Stadium
BTS
Allegiant Stadium
May 23, 24, 27 & 28, 2026
BTS returns to Las Vegas for four nights at Allegiant Stadium as part of their first worldwide group tour since the 2021–2022 "Permission to Dance on Stage" run. The biggest K-pop stadium tour in history — 34 regions, 79 shows — with a 360-degree in-the-round stage placing the audience at the center of the experience. Four nights in Las Vegas.
★ Historic Residency
LISA: VIVA LA LISA
The Colosseum at Caesars Palace
Nov 13, 14, 27 & 28, 2026
LISA becomes the first K-pop artist in history to perform a Las Vegas residency, taking over The Colosseum at Caesars Palace for four dates in November. A genuine milestone — not just for K-pop, but for Las Vegas entertainment. Four nights only.

Metallica at Sphere. BTS at Allegiant Stadium. LISA making Las Vegas history. Three of the biggest entertainment announcements of 2026 landing in a single week.
