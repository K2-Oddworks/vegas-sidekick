About the Show

What to Expect

Scott "Carrot Top" Thompson has been making Las Vegas lose its mind since 2005, and the Luxor's Atrium Showroom has been his home ever since. This is one of the longest-running comedy residencies on the Las Vegas Strip — and it earns that title every single night.

The format is deceptively simple: trunk after trunk of custom-built props, delivered at a pace that doesn't let the audience breathe. One bit transitions into the next before you've finished laughing. He's fast, physical, self-aware — and he doesn't need a long setup because the prop IS the punchline. Think observational humor meets George Carlin, wrapped in the chaos of Gallagher, with a streak of dark wit that keeps you off-balance in the best way.

The show evolves constantly. If you saw him years ago, the material has changed. If you've never seen prop comedy done at this level, this is the one. No bad seats in the Atrium Showroom — every ticket puts you right in the middle of it.

Frequently Asked

The show is recommended for ages 16 and up. There's adult language and mature humor throughout. Teenagers who appreciate comedy will likely love it — younger kids won't follow most of the references and will be restless.
The show runs approximately 75 minutes with no intermission. It moves fast — you won't be watching the clock. Plan to arrive at the Luxor property at least 45 minutes before showtime to give yourself time to navigate to the venue, and aim to be in your seat about 30 minutes before curtain.
There are no bad seats in the Atrium Showroom. It's an intimate venue with excellent sightlines throughout. That said, better seats fill up first — booking early gives you the most options. Front and center puts you closest to the props, which adds to the experience.
Carrot Top performs Monday through Saturday at 8:00 PM. He occasionally takes nights off — always verify availability at checkout. If tickets are showing for your date, the show is on.
Yes — a Meet & Greet Experience is available as a separate add-on ticket. It takes place before the show at 6:55 PM and includes a personal meeting and photo opportunity with Carrot Top. This is purchased separately from your show ticket. Check availability at checkout.
