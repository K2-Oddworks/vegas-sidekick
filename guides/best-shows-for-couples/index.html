Date-Night Shows — FAQ

What's the most romantic show in Las Vegas?

O by Cirque du Soleil at the Bellagio — its aquatic, dreamlike staging in a 1.5-million-gallon pool is the most romantic production on the Strip. Awakening at the Wynn is the runner-up.

What's a good date-night show that isn't too expensive?

Zombie Burlesque ($51), Mad Apple ($56), and Shin Lim ($67) all make excellent, affordable date nights. Mystère ($68) is the best-value Cirque du Soleil date.

What are the best adults-only shows for couples?

Absinthe and Atomic Saloon Show (both raunchy comedy-circus productions), plus the sexier revues Rouge and Zombie Burlesque. All are 18+ and lean funny and steamy rather than explicit.

Which Cirque du Soleil show is best for a date?

O for romance, Mad Apple for a fun night out with cocktails, Mystère for value, and KÀ or Michael Jackson ONE for pure spectacle.

What's the best show for a couple's first time in Vegas?

Mystère or Michael Jackson ONE — both are crowd-pleasing, high-energy, and hard not to love together. For a splurge, O by Cirque du Soleil.
