Vegas Magic Shows — FAQ

What's the best magic show in Vegas?

The two most acclaimed are AGT champions Mat Franco and Shin Lim. Mat Franco is the best all-around pick — warm, funny, interactive stage magic. Shin Lim is unmatched for close-up card magic. For sheer spectacle, Criss Angel MINDFREAK has the biggest production.

Who is the best magician in Las Vegas?

It's subjective, but the two America's Got Talent winners with their own Strip theaters — Mat Franco and Shin Lim — top most lists, with Penn & Teller the enduring legends at 21-plus years and counting.

What's the cheapest magic show in Vegas?

Nathan Burton Comedy Magic at $28 is the cheapest, followed by Allstars of Magic ($31) and An R-Rated Magic Show ($34) — all genuinely good, not just cheap.

What's the best magic show for families and kids?

Mac King Comedy Magic (an afternoon show, ages 5+) and Nathan Burton Comedy Magic are the easy family picks. For older kids who love big illusions, Now You See Me LIVE at the MGM Grand is a great choice.

Shin Lim or Mat Franco — which is better?

Shin Lim is silent, intense, world-record close-up card magic on big screens. Mat Franco is talkative, funny, and interactive stage magic. Card purists should pick Shin Lim; first-timers who want to laugh should pick Mat Franco.
