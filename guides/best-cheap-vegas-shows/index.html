Cheap Vegas Shows — FAQ

What's the cheapest show in Las Vegas?

The cheapest shows on the Strip start at $28. Marc Savard Comedy Hypnosis and Nathan Burton Comedy Magic are tied for the lowest ticket on this list, and both run about 70 minutes — so you're not sacrificing much show for the low price.

Are cheap Vegas shows actually any good?

Yes. Price in Vegas is mostly about venue size and production budget, not quality. A $30 magic or comedy show in an intimate theater can be every bit as entertaining as a $150 arena spectacle — it's just closer and smaller. Every show on this list is one we'd send our own friends to.

What's the cheapest magic show in Vegas?

Nathan Burton Comedy Magic at $28 is the cheapest, followed by Allstars of Magic ($31) and The Mentalist ($40). For families, Nathan Burton and Mac King are the easy picks.
